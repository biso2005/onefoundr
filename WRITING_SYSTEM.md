## BRAND VOICE
- Professional but approachable
- Practical and action-oriented
- Honest about challenges (not toxic positivity)
- "I've been there" tone, not "let me teach you"
- Short sentences mixed with flowing ones
- Uses real examples and data
- Never condescending or preachy

## FORBIDDEN PHRASES (PERMANENT)
- "delve into"
- "in conclusion" / "in summary"
- "it's important to note"
- "in today's world" / "in today's landscape"
- "when it comes to"
- "the fact of the matter"
- "at the end of the day"
- "that said" (at paragraph starts)
- "it's worth noting"
- "to be clear"
- "the truth is"
- "here's the reality"
- "dive deep"
- "unpack"
- "game changer"
- "unlock"
- "leverage" (as a verb)
- "solopreneur journey" (overused in niche)
- "hustle" (against our philosophy)
- "grind" (against our philosophy)
