## ARTICLE OUTLINE

### 1. HOOK: Generic Cold Emails Are Dead (250-300 words)
**Opening:** "Generic cold emails have a 2% response rate. But personalized cold emails? 15-25%. Here's how AI closes the gap."

- Problem: Personalization takes hours (30 min per email)
- Solution: AI personalization takes 5 min per email
- Proof: Data on response rates (generic vs personalized)
- Promise: "By end of this article, you'll have a system to send 50 personalized cold emails/week"

### 2. WHY GENERIC COLD OUTREACH FAILS (400-500 words)

**The problem solopreneurs face:**
- Manual outreach = 30 min per email (too slow)
- Template outreach = 2% response rate (doesn't work)
- Sales reps cost $40K+ (not possible as solopreneur)
- Catch-22: Need customers to survive, but getting customers takes all your time

**Data + Evidence:**
- 84% of cold emails never get opened
- Personalized cold emails: 2-8x higher response rate
- Founders responding to cold emails allocate $100K+ in deals after 1 warm touch

**Why this matters:** Cold outreach is still the #1 way solopreneurs get customers (despite what SaaS likes to claim)

### 3. THE AI COLD OUTREACH FRAMEWORK (1,200-1,500 words)

**STEP 1: BUILD YOUR TARGET LIST (With AI Research)**
- Use AI to identify 100-200 ideal customers
- Tools: Perplexity, ChatGPT (scrape LinkedIn), Crunchbase API
- AI extracts: Name, company, role, pain point, email
- Time: 2 hours with AI (vs 20 hours manually)

**STEP 2: RESEARCH EACH PROSPECT (AI Does 90%)**
- Prompt: "What's this person's business problem based on their recent posts, job change, or company news?"
- AI reads: LinkedIn, Twitter, recent news, company website
- Output: 1-paragraph summary per prospect
- Time: 30 sec per prospect (AI) vs 5 min (manual)

**STEP 3: WRITE YOUR ANGLE (This is the hard part—you do it)**
- Define 3-5 unique value propositions for your offer
- Choose 1 angle per prospect (based on their situation)
- Example angles:
  * Pain-based: "I help [roles] with [specific problem]"
  * Opportunity-based: "Your [situation] is perfect for [solution]"
  * Social proof-based: "Your peer [company name] just did [result]"

**STEP 4: WRITE PERSONALIZED EMAILS (AI Does First Draft)**
- Use ChatGPT/Claude with this structure:
  * Subject line (specific to prospect)
  * Hook (why you're reaching out + why them specifically)
  * Value (what you do + proof it works)
  * CTA (specific ask)
  * Signature (friendly, not corporate)

- Template (AI fills in [brackets]):
```
Subject: [Specific reference to their situation/recent news]

Hi [Name],

[Personal observation about their company/role + why they matter]

Most [roles] struggle with [problem]. We help them [outcome].

[Recent customer result from similar company/role]

Would be worth a 20-min call to see if something similar could work for you?

Best,
[Your name]
```

**STEP 5: SCALE THE OUTREACH (Automate It)**
- Tools: Gmail + Zapier, or Mailshake + ChatGPT
- Send: 5-10 personalized emails/day (rest personalization, not volume)
- Track: Opens, clicks, replies in spreadsheet
- Time: 30 minutes for 50 emails (write angle, AI personalizes, automate send)

### 4. 7 MISTAKES SOLOPRENEURS MAKE (400-500 words)

1. **Generic subject lines** — Personalize the SUBJECT first
2. **Too long emails** — Aim for 50-75 words
3. **No specific CTA** — Don't ask for "coffee." Ask for "20-min call Tuesday at 2 PM?"
4. **Hitting "reply all"** — Send individual emails, not bulk
5. **Same tone as competitors** — Stand out with personality
6. **Not following up** — 80% of deals close in follow-up #3-5
7. **Overlooking spam filters** — Short paragraphs, no links, personalization flags

### 5. YOUR AI COLD OUTREACH PLAYBOOK (600-700 words)

**Week 1: Research & Build List**
- Define ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) — 1 sentence
- Identify 100-200 prospects using LinkedIn (manual) or AI scraper
- Export to spreadsheet with name, email, company, role, recent activity

**Week 2: AI Research Phase**
- For each prospect: "Tell me this person's challenges based on their job title + company"
- Use Otter.ai or Notion AI to summarize
- Add 1-paragraph summary per prospect to spreadsheet

**Week 3: Write Your Angles**
- Write 3-5 different value propositions (based on their situations)
- Test subject lines (write 5, you pick best)
- Draft email template (60-80 words)

**Week 4: Personalization at Scale**
- Set up ChatGPT + Zapier workflow (or use Mailshake)
- For each prospect:
  * AI fills in name, company, specific reference
  * You review (2 sec per email)
  * Send
- Send 50 emails over 2 weeks (5-7 per day)

**Month 2: Follow-up & Iterate**
- Track opens/clicks/replies (in spreadsheet)
- Send follow-up #2 to non-responders (5-7 days later)
- Send follow-up #3 to non-responders (14 days later)
- Iterate based on what works
