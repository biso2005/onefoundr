# 20 Templates Every Solopreneur Needs to Run Their Business

You shouldn't be building the wheel every time. These are the 20 core templates that save you time and protect you legally.

---

## Financial Templates

### 1. Invoice Template

Use for every client project. Include:
- Your logo and contact info
- Invoice number and date
- Client details
- Itemized services with rates
- Due date
- Payment instructions

### 2. Proposal Template

Use before projects start. Include:
- Client goals (what they're trying to achieve)
- Scope (what you'll deliver)
- Timeline
- Investment/pricing
- Next steps

### 3. Expense Tracker

Track what you spend so you can deduct it from taxes:
- Date
- Category (software, equipment, etc.)
- Amount
- Business purpose

### 4. Monthly Profit & Loss Statement

Track revenue minus expenses to see if you're profitable.

### 5. Payment Agreement

Simple contract for payment terms.

---

## Client Templates

### 6. Client Onboarding Checklist

Automate your process:
- What info to collect from them
- What you send them
- Timeline for delivery
- Check-in cadence

### 7. Project Brief Template

Confirm understanding before starting:
- Goals
- Scope
- Timeline
- Budget
- Success metrics

### 8. Welcome Email Sequence

Send automatically to new clients:
- Email 1: Welcome + next steps
- Email 2: How to get most value
- Email 3: [Your product/service] overview

### 9. Follow-Up Email Template

When a prospect goes quiet:
- Acknowledge time
- Offer value
- Clear call to action

### 10. Testimonial Request Template

Ask clients for feedback:
- What transformation happened?
- What was specific about working together?
- What results did you get?

---

## Content Templates

### 11. Blog Post Template

Structure for fast writing:
- Hook (problem)
- Credibility (why listen to you)
- Main content (solution)
- Call to action

### 12. Email Newsletter Template

Share value weekly:
- Opening (personal/story)
- Main content (useful)
- Insight (framework/lesson)
- CTA (next step)

### 13. Social Media Post Templates

Platform-specific:
- LinkedIn: Thread starter + 3-4 follow-ups
- Twitter: Hot take + data/example + ask
- Instagram: Visual + 3-line caption + CTA

---

## Business Templates

### 14. Service Menu Template

List your offers and pricing clearly.

### 15. Scope of Work Template

Detailed agreement of what's included/excluded in your service.

### 16. Retainer Agreement

If you have monthly clients, specify:
- Monthly fee
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Cancellation terms

### 17. Confidentiality/NDA

Protect client secrets.

### 18. Contract Boilerplate

Basic agreement to use with all clients.

---

## Planning Templates

### 19. 90-Day Goal Setting

Plan quarterly:
- Revenue goal
- Content plan
- Client targets
- Learning goals

### 20. Weekly Review Checklist

Every Friday, assess:
- What worked this week?
- What didn't?
- What's next week?
- Do I need to adjust?

## Template Customization Rules

1. Always add your branding
2. Customize language to match your voice
3. Update prices/rates
4. Have a lawyer review contracts (if possible)
5. Test with one real use before mass using

Start with these 20. Customize them for your business. They'll save you hours per week.
