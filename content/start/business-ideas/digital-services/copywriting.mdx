## The Opportunity

Copywriters charge some of the highest rates in the freelance world because good copy directly impacts revenue. A sales page that converts at 3% instead of 1% is worth thousands to a business — so they'll pay premium rates for results.

## The Economics

**Beginner rate:** $50-100/hour
**Expert rate:** $3,000-10,000+ per project
**Monthly recurring:** $2,500-5,000 with 3-5 retainer clients

## Real Example

Marcus spent 90 days building a portfolio of 5 sales page projects by cold-emailing SaaS founders. His first 3 projects were at $1,500. By month 6, he was charging $5,000 per project and booked out 8 weeks ahead.

He now has 4 retainer clients at $3,000/month each, generating $12K monthly with focused 25-hour work weeks on sales pages that convert.

## What Makes Money

- **Sales pages** ($2,000-10,000 per page)
- **Email sequences** ($1,500-5,000 per sequence)
- **Landing pages** ($1,500-8,000 per page)
- **Product descriptions** ($500-2,000 per product line)
- **Retainer clients** ($2,000-5,000/month for ongoing updates)

## Getting Started

1. Write 3 sample sales pages for free (ideally for people you know)
2. Target one specific niche (SaaS founders, e-commerce, coaches)
3. Send cold emails to 20 prospects in that niche
4. Land 1-2 projects at lower rates to build case studies
5. Raise rates 30-50% with each new testimonial

## Skills You Need

- Understanding direct response copywriting (not brand writing)
- Ability to interview clients about their customers
- Basic psychology and persuasion
- HTML/basic design knowledge (optional, can partner with designers)

## Time to First Client

**3-4 weeks** with active outreach. Most people land their first client within 30 days if they prospect consistently.

## Scale Plan

Once profitable:
- Increase rates to $150-250/hour or $5K-15K per project
- Build a waiting list
- Add email/sales funnel copy as secondary offering
- Consider productizing your service with fixed scope
