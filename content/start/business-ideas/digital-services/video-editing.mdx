## The Opportunity

Every creator and podcast needs edited videos. Supply is low, demand is high, and you can start with software you may already have (Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve).

## The Economics

**Beginner:** $25-75 per video
**Experienced:** $500-2,000/month retainer (managing content calendar)
**Top tier:** $3,000-5,000+/month full content team for creators with 100K+ audiences

## The Model

Most video editors work on retainer:
- **Per-video model:** Quote $100-300 per edited YouTube video; manage 3-4 clients = $1,500-4,000/month
- **Retainer model:** $1,000-3,000/month to edit all videos for a creator (usually 4-8 videos/month)
- **Scale:** 5-10 retainer clients = $5K-30K/month

## Real Timeline

Month 1: Land 1 client through outreach
Month 2-3: Build portfolio, land 2-3 more clients
Month 4: 4-5 clients at $800-1,500/month each = $3,200-7,500/month

## What You Need

- Editing software ($20-80/month for Adobe suite)
- Decent computer (anything from last 3-5 years works)
- Portfolio of 3-5 edited videos (edit for free if needed)

## Getting Started

1. Specialize: Choose ONE type (YouTube edits, Reels, podcasts)
2. Build portfolio: Edit 3 free videos for friends/other creators
3. Pitch to creators: Find 20 YouTube channels in your niche, DM the creator
4. Land first paid gig at $25-50 per video
5. Systematize: Create templates, keyboard shortcuts, workflows

## Scale Plan

- Bundle video editing + thumbnail design + captions
- Offer "content batching" (edit month's worth of content at once)
- Build a team of junior editors under you
- Partner with videographers or on-camera talents
