## The Opportunity

Every business needs social media presence. Most entrepreneurs hate creating content. This creates a perfect market for someone who can manage 5-8 accounts systematically.

## The Economics

**Entry rate:** $1,500-2,000/month per client (5-8 hours/week content creation, posting, engagement)
**Standard rate:** $3,000-5,000/month (include analytics reporting and strategy adjustments)
**Scale:** 6-8 systematized clients = $18K-40K/month

## The Model

Work with 1-2 industries deep (e.g., coaches, B2B SaaS, local services). This lets you:
- Reuse content themes and templates
- Understand the market deeply
- Create better content faster
- Charge premium rates because you get better results

## Real Math

**Month 1-2:** Land 2 clients at $1,500/month = $3,000
**Month 3-4:** Land 2 more clients = $6,000/month
**Month 5-6:** Land 2 more clients = $9,000/month
**Month 6+:** 6 clients at $1,500-2,500 each = $9K-15K/month

## What You Need

- Content management tools (Buffer, Later, Meta Business Suite)
- Canva or similar for graphics
- Understanding of platform algorithms
- Ability to write engaging copy

## The Secret: Batching

Solopreneurs who batch content work is the key:
- Spend Monday-Tuesday creating 30 posts for the month
- Wednesday-Thursday scheduling everything
- Thursday-Friday organizing/reporting
- One 30-minute check-in call with client per week

This lets you manage 8-10 clients with 20-25 hours/week.

## Getting Started

1. Choose your niche (coaches, SaaS, local services)
2. Build a portfolio: Manage your own account or do 1-2 free trial months
3. Pitch to 30 prospects in your niche
4. Land first client at $1,500-2,000/month
5. Document your process (become more efficient each month)

## Scale Plan

- Add strategy consulting ($500-1,000/month premium)
- Include paid ads management (+$1,000-2,000/month)
- Build a content calendar template specific to your niche
- Hire VA to handle posting/basic engagement while you handle strategy
